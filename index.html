About Me

Hi, I'm Alex! I am a mechanical engineer with 5+ years of experience across a wide range of industries where I was involved heavily in R&D and manufacturing processes. I'm passionate about structural and fluidic design, and I have a strong sense for developing unconventional models in CAD.
